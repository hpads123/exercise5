Camotes

Aside from the beautful beaches mainland Cebu has to offer, the Camotes islands boasts its wonderful and breath-taking beaches that seems to be untouched by the people. Located within the vicinity of Cebu province, these 4 islands of Poro, Tulang, Pacijan and Ponson, provides a great vacation for people who likes to spend their vacation days near magnificent bodies of water.

Osmena Peak

A well-known mountain located in Dalaguete, Cebu, popular for its semi-deformed jagged hills with low temperatures and surrounded by pine trees, gardens and woodhouses inhabited by the locals. This is one of the most wanted places to be if you are into hiking as if gives you a challenge to climb to it's peak that offers peaceful view of southern part of Cebu.

Sirao Garden

Dubbed as the "Little Amsterdam of Cebu", Sirao Flower Garden is the home of many colorful. It attracts a lot of people, local or foreign, and gives them "Dutch rural vibes" amidst it's relative short distance from the city as they stroll around the beatiful flowers planted on the area.

The area is also near Taoist Temple and Temple of Leah, all are located in Busay, Cebu City

Kawasan Falls

The Kawasan Falls, located in Badian, Cebu, is on of the best places to visit, especially for nature-loving tourists. It has been popular to people, especially if you are a fan of canyooneering activities. Looking from the top below, it gives a refreshing sight by looking at its blue waters and cool natural pools. It has been reviewed as one of the best waterfalls in the Philippines because of its terrain (three-level waterfall) that is also a site for other water activities aside from canyoneering.
